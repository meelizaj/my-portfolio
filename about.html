About me

Hi, I'm Meeliza.

I'm a creative with a deep love for virtual storytelling whether
through my lens, a song, or a moment of stillness I find inspiration
in quiet walks, soulful music, and the rhythm of everyday's life.
Jazz, indie pop, and R&B are my soundtracks, and they often
influence the way I see and frame the world.

Over the years, I've developed a style rooted in authenticity and
emotion. From portraits to lifestyle's shoots, I aim to capture more than just
an image—I want to tell a story. With an eye for details and a heart for
coonection, I bring both creativity and intention into every project I take
on.

I believe creating with purpose, working closely with clients or
independently to deliver work that feels honest, polished, and human.
Whether I'm holding a camera or strumming a chord, I'm always
chasing means in the details.
